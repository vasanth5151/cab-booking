Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book a ride?

You can book a ride through our website, mobile app, or by calling our customer service line. Simply enter your pickup and drop-off locations, select your preferred vehicle type, and confirm your booking.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), debit cards, and popular digital wallets like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your booking up to 5 minutes before the scheduled pickup time without any charges. Cancellations made after this time may incur a small fee.

Are your drivers licensed and insured?

All our drivers are professionally licensed, background-checked, and fully insured. We maintain strict safety standards and provide regular training to ensure your safety and comfort.

Do you offer corporate accounts?

Yes, we offer corporate accounts with special rates, centralized billing, and dedicated account management. Contact our business team at business@rideeasy.com for more information.

What areas do you serve?

We currently serve over 50 cities across the United States. You can check if we operate in your area by entering your location on our booking form or contacting customer service.
